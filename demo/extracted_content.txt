Welcome to Travel Africa magazine

Travel Africa is a quarterly journal that celebrates all that we love about the world’s most exciting continent, helping you to plan and get the most out of your visits. Published since 1997, we draw on the insight and experience of travel experts, writers, photographers, conservationists and travellers to provide inspiration and advice, whether you are new to Africa or have already fallen under its spell. With the turn of every page, discover something new about the continent’s diverse attractions, wildlife and culture, and immerse yourself in safari life — those unforgettable moments that touch the soul and keep us returning. Join our wonderful community of African travellers. Welcome! Karibu!

Issue 1112 (June-August 2026)

In a busy world, we owe it to ourselves to seek moments of peace and quiet. And is there anywhere that offers opportunities to do this more abundantly than Africa? Not only does it exude space and solitude, but its natural rhythm slows you down and reconnects you with the restorative power of nature. In our new issue, we explore places and experiences that encourage you to slow down, breathe deeply and luxuriate in space and emptiness. From Kafue to Cederberg and Morocco to Saadani, there’s plenty to inspire your next peaceful escape.
